About the Workshop

The BHS AI Training Workshop is a full-day, interactive experience designed to give high school students a real understanding of artificial intelligence — how it works, where it's used, and how you can use it responsibly. Whether you're curious about chatbots, machine learning, or the future of technology, this workshop will give you practical knowledge and skills you can apply right away.

What You'll Learn

🧠

What is AI?

Understand the basics of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and how modern AI systems are trained.

💬

AI Tools in the Real World

Explore how AI is used in education, healthcare, business, and daily life — with live demos.

🛠

Hands-On Practice

Work through guided activities using real AI tools. Create, experiment, and problem-solve.

⚖

AI Ethics & Responsibility

Discuss bias, misinformation, and how to be a thoughtful, responsible AI user.

🚀

AI & Your Future

Learn how AI skills can open doors to careers in technology, science, arts, and beyond.

👥

Collaborative Challenges

Team up with classmates to tackle a real AI-powered challenge and present your solution.

Who Should Attend

This workshop is open to all BHS students — no tech background needed. You're a great fit if you are:

  • Curious about technology and how AI works
  • Interested in future careers in STEM or tech
  • A student who uses AI tools and wants to use them better
  • Someone who wants to understand AI's impact on society
  • Looking for a fun, hands-on learning experience
  • Any grade level — freshmen through seniors welcome
